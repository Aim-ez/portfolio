More About Me

Where I Started

I've been programming since 2017, when a high school computer science class introduced me to game development. Somewhere between chasing down my first impossible bug and finally watching the program work, I was hooked. That feeling of spending hours searching for a single mistake, only to fix it and see everything come together, is still one of my favourite parts of development.

Where I am Now

Today, I'm a recent Computer Science graduate working as a Network Coordinator for Elections Manitoba. While I appreciate the experience I've gained in IT, my passion has always been software development. I see technology as a creative medium that allows me to turn ideas into experiences people can actually use.

What I Enjoy Building

I've felt drawn towards full-stack development because I love building clean, intuitive interfaces just as much as I enjoy designing the databases behind them. Experimentation, simplicity, attention to detail, and good user experiences are the things I try to bring into every project, even if it means learning something completely new along the way.

Featured Project

One of my favourite projects is PlotLine, a social reading app where users can build profiles, review books, follow friends, and discover what others are reading. I designed and built the user interface, developed the database backend, integrated the Google Books API, and implemented authentication and user profiles. Watching it evolve from an idea into a full application reminded me why I enjoy software development so much.

Outside of Tech

Outside of programming, you'll usually find me with a book in my hand, singing, dancing, playing a sport, or planning my next trip. I love stories in all their forms, from classic television and story-based video games to the places I've been fortunate enough to explore around the world. Those experiences shaped how I think about software, too: the best products aren't just functional, they're the ones that connect with people.

Looking Ahead

I'm excited to start my career as a software developer and continue learning from experienced teammates while contributing to products that make a difference. If you think we'd work well together, I'd love to connect.
